Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using Tikdown

How do I download a TikTok video without watermark?
Simply copy the link of the video from the TikTok app, paste it into the search box above, and click Download. Wait for our server to process it, then choose the 'No Watermark' option.
Is it free to use?
Yes, Tikdown is 100% free to use. You can download as many videos as you want without any restrictions or hidden fees.
Does it support Photo Slides?
Absolutely. Our system auto-detects if the link is a video or an image carousel. You can download individual images from the slide or the background music.
Where are the videos saved?
Videos are saved to your device's default download folder, usually 'Downloads' on Windows, Mac, Android, and iPhone.
